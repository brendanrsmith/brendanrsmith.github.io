Personal SMS avalanche forecast service.

## How it works

I text a zone keyword (like `baker` or `stevens`) and forecast type to my AvyBot number and receive the current avalanche danger forecast from the Northwest Avalanche Center (NWAC) in reply.

## Consent

Messages are only sent in direct response to inbound SMS requests. No unsolicited messages are ever sent.
