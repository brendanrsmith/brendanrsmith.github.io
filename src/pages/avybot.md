SMS avalanche forecast service for the Pacific Northwest.

## How it works

Text a zone keyword to receive current avalanche danger ratings and conditions.

**Supported zones:** `baker`, `stevens`, `snoqualmie`, `olympics`, `hood`, and more.

**Commands:**

- `a baker` — avalanche forecast for Mt Baker area
- `a stevens 2` — two-day outlook for Stevens Pass
- `help` — list all zones and commands

## Example

**Request:** `a baker`

**Response:**

```
West Slopes North — Feb 24
Above: Consid | Near: Consid | Below: Mod

Storm Slab: Likely, D1.5-2.5
All aspects, all elevs

Unstable snow could linger on some slopes.
```

## Consent & Opt-In

**By sending a text message to AvyBot, you expressly consent to receive SMS text messages in response.**

These messages contain avalanche danger ratings and conditions summaries.

**How to opt in:** Send any valid zone keyword (e.g., `baker`, `stevens`) to the AvyBot number.

**How to opt out:** Text `STOP` at any time to stop receiving messages.

**Message frequency:** You only receive messages in direct response to your requests. No automated, scheduled, or marketing messages are ever sent.

**Message and data rates may apply.**
